Welcome to the Unode open-source community! Here, you can exchange experiences and share ideas with other developers.

## 📜 Group Rules

To maintain a positive community atmosphere, please adhere to the following rules:

<Callout type="warn" title="Important Notice">
  Violation of the following rules may result in removal from the chat group. Please read and adhere to them carefully.
</Callout>

1.  Group administrators and owners are not obligated to provide any technical support. If you have questions, please submit an [issue](/en/docs/support/feedback-issues)
2.  Chat content should focus on the use, deployment, development, and feedback of Unode's open-source project. Do not post information related to account trading, purchase guidance, or solicitations.
3.  This group is solely for open-source community communication. Please obtain project information through official repositories, documentation, and community channels.
4.  Unode adopts the GNU AGPLv3 open-source license. It can be used for free as long as the license is complied with. For details, please refer to [Project Introduction](/en/docs/guide/wiki/basic-concepts/project-introduction)
5.  When using this project, you should comply with relevant laws and regulations, upstream terms of service, platform rules, and content security requirements.
6.  Community discussions should revolve around legal authorization, compliant deployment, and legitimate engineering issues. Related messages and group member management will be handled according to community rules.

<Callout title="Friendly Reminder">
  If you have any feature suggestions or bug feedback, it is recommended to submit an issue directly on GitHub, which allows for better tracking and resolution of problems.
</Callout>
